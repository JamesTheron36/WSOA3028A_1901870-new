The Promise and Peril of User Generated Content

This portal into the digital space provides the user with a connection into a reality of ultimate communication improving upon the predecessor of sending notes attached to pigeons (or owls in alternative realities with a less developed understanding of magic). A multiverse of culture and industry forms through the practices which take place through the manifestation of bits in the form of waves in the visible and audible spectrum.
